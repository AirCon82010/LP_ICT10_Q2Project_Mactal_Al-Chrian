About Our School

The Kontrabida Academy film is a comedy and satire that chronicles the training of aspiring actors who enroll in an elite school dedicated to mastering the exaggerated art of movie villainy in the Philippines. It's a hilarious tribute to the iconic antagonists of Filipino cinema.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and innovative curriculum create an environment where students can thrive in the fields of dramatic confrontation, power posing, and emotional devastation.

Vision

To be the ultimate breeding ground for cinema's most effective and dramatically powerful antagonists, ensuring global domination of the spotlight.

Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a learning environment that promotes over-the-top melodrama, impeccable fashion sense, and the calculated destruction of the protagonist's dreams.
